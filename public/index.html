  • infoWhat is this?

    SoMeQR is short for:
    (So)cial (Me)dia (Q)uick (R)esponse.

    The purpose of this APP is to make a QR code that links to a social media Profile.
    This allows you to connect with people you meet in person, alot quicker.
    Alternatively you would have to tell the person what your profile name is and have them search for your info in the respective Social Media App.

    Or you could spend money on business cards that you keeps forgetting when you need to exchange information fast!

    With a SoMeQR code on your smartphone lock screen - other smartphone users can simply activate their camera app and hold it over your lock screen, to go directly to your choosen Social Media URL.

    N.B. Some camera APP's require you to do something extra to activate QR codes:

    • phone_iphoneiOS 11+ - Just open the camera APP and place the QR Code in the viewfinder, a notification will take you to the URL in the QR Code.
    • phone_androidAndroid 6+ - Open the camera APP, look at the QR Code in the viewfinder, long tap on “Home Button” or middle button at the bottom, it’ll scan the QR Code instantly.
    • device_unknownOlder / Other need a QR Code Scanner APP. Just search the Play or App Store.

  • help_outlineHow to

    Touch the QR-Code image to activate the generator.
    Then type in the URL to your personal Social Media Profile and Generate a QR code Image.

    Once Done, take a screen grab of the code, and set it as your lock screen image on your mobile.
    Now you can share your profile with anyone who opens up their camera app, and holds it over your lock screen!
    Or put it in the corner of your PowerPoint slides, so audiences can connect with you!
    (Requires QR recognition in mobile camera app - most phones have this))
